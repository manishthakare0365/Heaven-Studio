Common Questions

Answered Before You Ask.

Where is Heaven Studio located?

We are located at D-99, Sector 122, Noida. You can get directions on Google Maps here. The studio is easily accessible via Metro (Sector 101, Aqua Line) and from the Greater Noida Expressway.

How do I book a session?

Fill out the booking form on this page or WhatsApp us at +91 87777 59923. We'll discuss your requirements, suggest the best package, and confirm your date with a small advance.

When will I receive my edited photos?

Edited images are delivered within 7–10 business days via a private, password-protected online gallery. High resolution and print-ready. Rush delivery within 3–4 days is available for select packages.

Do you offer packages for multiple services?

Absolutely. Many clients combine Maternity + Newborn or Pre-Wedding + Wedding coverage. We offer bundled packages at preferential rates — reach out and we'll create a custom quote.

Do you shoot at outdoor or home locations in Noida?

Yes — we regularly shoot at outdoor locations across Noida, Greater Noida, and Delhi NCR, as well as in-home sessions. Location scouting, travel, and setup are all handled by our team. A travel fee may apply beyond a certain radius.
